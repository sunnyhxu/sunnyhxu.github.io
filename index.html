Projects

Math projects

  • Algebriac Combinatorics with Nathan Phan, advised by Ethan Partida, Brown University Directed Reading Program in Spring 2025.

CS projects

  • Using Intra-Stock, Inter-Stock, and Temporal Aggregation to Predict Stock Market Returns and Stock Selections, with Prottoya Chowdhury, Bailing Hou, and Devesh Kumar, final project for Brown CSCI1470 Deep Learning.

    • The project will re-implement the MASTER model by Li et al. 2024, a market-guided stock transformer model, and train on the Global Factor data to predict US stock prices. Github repo.

  • PatMu, with Melody Chen and Joanne Ding, Best First-time Hack at Hack@Brown 2025.

    • Patmu is an AR-powered web game in which users pat to create music. The game utilizes computer vision to detect movements and play virtual instruments like drums, piano chords, and melodic sequences. Github repo.

Research

  • Lunar Regolith Thickness Evaluation with Rocky and non-Rocky Ejecta Craters, advised by Dr. Andrea Rajšić, Brown University Undergraduate Teaching and Research Awards.

    • I mapped fresh craters on lunar surfaces and classified them based on their morphology. Calculating excavation depth with crater diameters, I estimated lunar regolith thickness and compared them with on-site seismic data on Apollo landing sites.
